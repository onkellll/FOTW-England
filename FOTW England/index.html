The Regency Cafe


Located in London’s Westminister, is a traditional English cafe that has been serving hearty British meals since 1946. Known for its “greasy spoon” charm, this cafe has become a beloved institution, frequented by locals, tourists, and even celebrities. It has appeared in numerous TV shows and films, adding to its iconic status.

Menu and prices


The Regency Cafe is famous for its Full English Breakfast, which typically includes two sausages, two rashers of bacon, baked beans, two toast, and a fried egg for £6.50.
